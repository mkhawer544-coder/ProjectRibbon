About Project Ribbon

Our Purpose

Project Ribbon was created to bridge the gap between breast cancer awareness and actionable support. We recognized that while awareness campaigns are important, people need comprehensive resources that address not just the medical aspects, but also the emotional, psychological, and practical challenges that come with a breast cancer diagnosis.

Why We Exist

Breast cancer doesn't just affect the bodyâ€”it impacts every aspect of life. We exist to provide holistic support that addresses the complete journey from awareness to recovery and beyond.

Our Team's Motivation

Our team is driven by personal experiences and a shared commitment to making a difference. We've seen how early detection and proper support can change outcomes, and we're dedicated to ensuring everyone has access to these resources.

What We Do

We provide educational resources about detection technology, connect people with mental health support, maintain a directory of local services, and build a community where survivors can share their stories and inspire others.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and understanding.

Empowerment

We provide knowledge and resources that enable people to take charge of their health.

Community

Hope

We spread positivity and optimism while acknowledging the challenges.
